Our Mission

More Than
A Tournament

The And-1 City Wide Hoopfest gives competitive basketball opportunities to middle school, high school, and adult players across the Philadelphia and Delaware County area. We combine structured competition with professional-level presentation and community impact.

Early rounds take place at local recreation centers, school gyms, and community courts — bringing the event to the neighborhoods that make Philadelphia great. The championship games move to Xfinity Mobile Arena, giving players a professional stage to compete on.

Who Can Play

Four
Divisions

From middle school exposure to adult competitive play, every level of basketball has a home at Hoopfest. No tournament in the region covers this range.

01
Middle School Exposure
A first look at organized competitive basketball in a professionally run environment. Designed to grow the regional pipeline and give younger athletes a pathway to follow.
02
Junior Varsity
Competitive tournament play for JV-level high school athletes. A step up in structure, officiating, and intensity from recreational leagues.
03
Varsity
The marquee high school division. Top teams from Philadelphia and Delaware County compete for championship honors at Xfinity Mobile Arena.
04
Adult Recreational
AAU and adult competitive teams round out the bracket. Structured, officiated competition for post-high school players who want to keep playing the game they love.
Championship Stage

Xfinity Mobile Arena

Wells Fargo Center Complex — South Philadelphia

Championship rounds elevate the tournament to a professional level. Competing where Philadelphia's professional teams play creates an atmosphere unlike any other local basketball event — and gives athletes a moment they will carry with them.

With the NBA season wrapped by summer, the arena sits available during precisely the months Hoopfest needs it. Our partnership model offers the facility a revenue-generating event during an otherwise vacant period.

  • Professional court surface and lighting
  • Full scoreboard and PA system
  • Seating for thousands of fans
  • Revenue-share agreement model with venue
  • Long-term partnership opportunity
Revenue Strategy

How We Build
A Sustainable Event

01
Ticket Sales
Dynamic pricing structure — earlier rounds and younger divisions carry lower ticket costs. Championship rounds at Xfinity Mobile Arena are priced to reflect the elevated experience.
02
Sponsorship & Advertising
Logo placement on jerseys and signage, branded swag bags, halftime activations, and social media promotions. Advertising slots across all rounds fund court rental costs directly.
03
Vendors, Merch & Raffles
Food vendor rentals, tournament merchandise sales, and daily and weekly raffles provide additional revenue streams. All vendor fees are structured to benefit both the event and participating small businesses.

Roots

Built for
Philadelphia

Basketball in Philadelphia is not just a sport — it is embedded in neighborhoods, schools, and communities across the city. The And-1 Hoopfest is designed to reflect those roots by hosting early rounds at local recreation centers and school gyms throughout the region.

Local food vendors, small businesses, and community organizations are central participants — not afterthoughts. Vendor booths, sponsorship partnerships, and promotional giveaways ensure that the economic activity of the tournament stays in the community.

Social media coverage on Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, and Facebook will amplify player highlights, local business features, and tournament information — extending the event's reach far beyond the courts themselves.

Behind the Event

Operations &
Leadership

01
Event Director
Oversees the full tournament structure, manages relationships with facilities, sponsors, and partners, and holds final accountability for event execution at every level.
02
Operations Manager
Responsible for game scheduling across all divisions and locations, referee coordination, volunteer management, and ensuring each facility is properly prepared for game day.
03
Marketing & Sponsorship Manager
Handles all social media promotion, sponsor relationship development, vendor coordination, and community outreach throughout the full tournament period.
04
Referee Program
Locally hired certified referees at every game. Fees are split between competing teams per game, reducing fixed operating costs for the event while maintaining fair, professional officiating.
05
Volunteer Network
Local college students, community members, and youth basketball organizations contribute to scorekeeping, team check-ins, ticket scanning, and crowd management throughout the tournament.
06
Digital Scheduling Hub
A centralized online scheduling system is updated live through official social media accounts, keeping players, coaches, and fans current on matchups, results, and venue information.
Gameday

The Fan
Experience

01
Live DJs and Local Music
Live music at outdoor venues supports Philadelphia and Delaware County bands and performers. A constant soundtrack runs throughout every game day at indoor facilities.
02
Halftime Shows
Local dance programs and cheerleading teams perform during halftime at select games — showcasing regional talent and giving fans a complete entertainment experience.
03
Raffles and Giveaways
Daily and weekly raffles feature tournament merchandise, restaurant gift cards, and other prizes. Promotional activations from sponsors add additional interactive moments throughout each day.
04
Merchandise Stand
On-site merchandise featuring both unbranded gear and premium And-1 Hoopfest branded apparel. Players, families, and fans can take something home from every tournament day.
Our Commitment

Inclusion, Ethics &
Social Impact

Fair competition, safety, and community access are non-negotiable. A clear code of conduct governs players, coaches, and spectators at every venue. Certified referees officiate every game. All facilities meet documented safety standards, and medical support is available at larger venues. Eligibility and roster rules are communicated transparently ahead of each division's bracket.
